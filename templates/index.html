Prompting Tips

  • Be specific with camera work (dolly, tracking, aerial, handheld)
  • Describe lighting explicitly (golden hour, neon-lit, overcast)
  • Set the scene first, then describe action
  • Include texture and material details
  • Specify motion and pacing (slow motion, timelapse, real-time)
  • Use style references (Wes Anderson, cyberpunk, film noir)
  • Keep one subject per clip for best coherence
  • Add a reference image for consistency across clips
